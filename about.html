About Us

We are here waiting for you

Bestie's bakers have been working in a cramped space for a few of years, preparing the Karangahape Road cafe's distinctive layer cakes, traditional slices, cinnamon scrolls, and vegan goodies, but they now have their own luxury new kitchen. Owners Emma Lyell and Tane Williams characterise their new outpost, Baby, as Bestie's cousin, with cabinet food, a tiny menu (including hoagies), and a small bar.
